🔒 Data Privacy & Processing

KAFO collects personal data for membership services, community placement, and programme matching.

  • Data: Name, Civil ID, contact, nationality, professional background, interests, assessment responses, documents.
  • Purpose: Member assessment, level placement, community matching, forum role identification, programme recommendations.
  • Storage: Secured in KAFO's internal systems. Accessible only to authorised staff.
  • Retention: Duration of membership + 12 months post-expiry. Deletion on request.
  • Sharing: Never sold or shared. Aggregated anonymous data used in ESG/impact reports.
  • Rights: Members may request access, correction, or deletion at any time.

Assessment results are for internal community matching only and are not disclosed in raw form.
